Q4 — Why does ICE say they're being held?
¿Por qué dice ICE que están detenidos?
ICE says they're held because of a criminal conviction.  Qualifying offenses: aggravated felony, drug trafficking, firearms, crimes of moral turpitude, crimes of violence.
ICE dice que están detenidos por una condena criminal.  Condenas que califican: delito agravado, tráfico de drogas, armas, crímenes de torpeza moral, crímenes de violencia.
Q5 — Does the conviction actually qualify for mandatory detention?
¿La condena califica para la detención obligatoria?
ICE says the person "arrived without inspection." If they were arrested INSIDE the US (not at a border), ICE may be misapplying the law — they may be entitled to a bond hearing NOW. (Yajure Hurtado, BIA 2025)
Si fueron arrestados DENTRO de EE.UU. (no en la frontera), ICE puede estar aplicando la ley incorrectamente — y pueden tener derecho a una audiencia de fianza AHORA.
